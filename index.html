About Me

I'm a software engineer and systems engineer working in the UK. I currently head up a cloud and IoT company called Hark where I design and build distributed systems and hardware to help companies monitor and store sensor data in real time. Prior to Hark, I lead the development on a search analytics platform at a leading agency in the North. I love technical challenges! I tend to regularly use C#, Go, PHP. This is a place for my own thoughts and experiences with big data and high performance applications.

While I love the idea of making this site look nicer, and I will, I want to be writing about things for now. Jekyll + Bootstrap will have to do!

You can tweet me, see my code, or read my articles on the Hark Labs blog.
